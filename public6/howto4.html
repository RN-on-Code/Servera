# Step 5:

Here we are left with the last layer of the Rubik's cube to be sorted. As you end up solving the second layer, you may encounter two types of patters formed on the top layer that is the yellow face of the cube. Viz ,

For Do:
F, R, U, R', U', F'
And you'll be directed to Pattern2

For Do:
F, R, U, R', U', F'
This will form a '+' pattern on the top layer. >>

However, once in a blue moon you may be directed to Pattern3 already as soon as you end up solving the second layer of the cube.
