# Step 7:

In this step you need to adjust the corners so they fit onto their positions. At this stage, you might encounter situations where only one corner is positioned, regardless of its arrangement, or none of them are positioned.

If you find any one corner positioned correctly; hold the cube in a such a way that the particular corner should be present on the right side of your front face. >> , and apply the following algorithm:
L', U, R, U', L, U, R', U'

(Logic: This algorithm moves the corner piece on the front-left to the back-left, the one on the back-left to the back-right, and the one on the back-right to the left-front. And the corner piece on the front-right stays still on its position.)

Repeat this algorithm until all the corner pieces are placed in their desired positions. >> ,

However, if not a single corner piece is correctly positioned intially; keeping the yellow face as the top one, and any face out of Red, Green, Blue, Orange in front and apply the same algorithm until you get any one corner piece positioned correctly.
